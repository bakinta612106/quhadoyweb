Governors Ball Releases 2019 Lineup Featuring Florence &amp; The Machine, SZA &amp; More

Publish date: 2024-09-08

The lineup for the ninth annual Governors Ball festival includes headliners Tyler The Creator, Florence & The Machine, and The Strokes.

From May 31 to June 2, artists such as Lily Allen, Bazzi, Jessie Reyez, Ty Dolla $ign, Vince Staples, Charlie XCX, Kasey Musgraves, the 1975, King Princess, Jorja Smith, Lil Wayne, and Brockhampton will perform in Randall’s Island in New York City. Last year, Eminem, Travis Scott, and Jack White headlined the festival.
