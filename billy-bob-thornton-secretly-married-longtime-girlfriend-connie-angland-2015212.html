Billy Bob Thornton Secretly Married Longtime Girlfriend Connie Angland

Publish date: 2024-09-03

He’s taken once more! Billy Bob Thornton secretly married his longtime girlfriend Connie Angland back in October, his rep confirms to Us Weekly.

The Fargo actor, 59, and Angland exchanged vows on Oct. 22 “at their home in Los Angeles during a private ceremony before family,” the rep says.

The couple have been together for over 12 years and welcomed daughter Bella in September 2004. Last month, he won a Golden Globe for Best Actor in a Mini-Series or Motion Picture Made for Television for Fargo — while noticeably wearing a silver wedding band. (Angland has worked in the special effects department for Men in Black and Planet of the Apes.)

This is the sixth marriage for Oscar winner Thornton, who was famously married to Angelina Jolie from 2000 to 2003. Jolie went on to marry Brad Pitt at their estate in the South of France in summer 2014, but revealed in January 2015 that they had legally wed earlier in California before their formal ceremony.

Thornton has also been married to Melissa Lee Gatlin (1978 to 1980), Toni Lawrence (1986 to 1988), Cynda Williams (1990 to 1992), and Pietra Thornton (1993 to 1997). He shares daughter Amanda with Gatlin and sons William and Harry with Thornton.
