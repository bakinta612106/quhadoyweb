Xbox One's Upload Studio just got a lot better

Publish date: 2024-08-17

The Xbox One's built-in video editing software, Upload Studio, recently got a pretty major update that should make it easier to edit and show off your game highlights.

Upload Studio now lets users piece together up to 15 segments from game clips, up to 30 minutes in total length. The editing tool also now comes with transitions, title cards, intros, effects and splash screens. Some of those transitions and effects include overly dramatic explosions while some are themed around games like Halo, Minecraft and Sunset Overdrive. Users can now also add text overlays and voiceover to their videos, straight from their Xbox One.

Better still, Xbox One owners can add green screen-style effects with Kinect, which is rife with goofy possibilities.
