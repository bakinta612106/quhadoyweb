Gregg Allman, 64, Engaged to 24-Year-Old

Publish date: 2024-08-21

Maybe the seventh time will be the charm?

Gregg Allman, the 64-year-old founding member of The Allman Brothers Band, revealed on Piers Morgan Tonight that he’s engaged to a 24-year-old woman named Shannon.

Allman has been married six times before and has five children: son Devon Allman, 39, with his ex-wife Shelley Kay Winters; son Elijah Blue Allman, 35, with ex-wife Cher; daughter Delilah Island Allman, 31, with ex-wife Julie Bindas; son Michael Sean Allman, 45, with ex-girlfriend Mary Lynn Green; and daughter Layla Brooklyn Allman with ex-girlfriend Shelby Blackburn, 19.

Piers Morgan asked Allman, “How does she feel about becoming wife number seven?”

“That’s not what she’s becoming. She’s becoming wife number one. I don’t have a wife, I haven’t had one for years,” Allman reasoned.
