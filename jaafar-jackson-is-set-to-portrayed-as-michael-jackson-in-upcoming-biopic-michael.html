Jaafar Jackson Is Set To Portrayed As Michael Jackson In Upcoming Biopic Michael

Publish date: 2024-08-31

Jaafar Jackson, Michael Jackson’s nephew, has been confirmed to play the late pop icon in the forthcoming biopic, Michael. Michael’s mother, Katherine, commended Jaafar as well, claiming he “embodies” her son. She went on to say:

“It’s fantastic to see him carrying on the Jackson heritage of artists and performers.”

Graham King, the film’s producer, revealed that he met Jaafar two years ago and was struck by how he portrayed Michael’s energy and charisma. King went on to say:

“It was something so strong that even after undertaking a global search, it was evident that he is the only individual to take on this job. I am overjoyed that he has agreed to play his uncle, and I can’t wait for the world to see him on the big screen as Michael Jackson.”

Antoine Fuqua, who is directing the picture, expressed his excitement to see Jaafar play Michael. He went on to say that during their first encounter, he had a spiritual connection with Jaafar and that the actor is always excellent on camera.
