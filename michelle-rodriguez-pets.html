Michelle Rodriguez

Birthday

July 12, 1978 (45)

Mayte Michelle Rodriguez, is an American actress, screenwriter, and DJ. Her breakout role was as a troubled boxer in the independent film Girlfight, which was met with critical acclaim and earned her several awards, including the Independent Spirit Award and Gotham Award for Best Debut Performance. The following year, she starred as Letty Ortiz in the blockbuster film The Fast and the Furious, reprising her role in its sequels Fast & Furious, Fast & Furious 6, and Furious 7 with Vin Diesel and Paul Walker. Michelle Rodriguez has a reported net worth of $30 million.
