25 Days of One Direction: A collection of our favorite TV looks

Publish date: 2024-08-16

One Direction gave their last TV performance on December 13th, 2015 on The X Factor UK. Now a year later, we are still patiently waiting to see them perform together again.

They have made a huge impact on so many people, including us, and we are celebrating with the “25 Days of One Direction” here at CelebMix. Today, we are taking a look back on some of the boys’ best outfits as seen on TV!

Louis

Honestly, we had so many favorite TV looks for Louis! From his suits, to his jackets, to his plain T-shirts, he looks handsome in everything. However, one of our favorites is definitely his look from the Teen Choice Awards.

Niall

We can’t get enough of Niall’s TV looks, especially when he is rocking out on his guitar! One of our personal favorites is his outfit when he performed on Good Morning America. It was a chilly day in New York City that morning, which is why he wore a few layers of jackets.

Liam

We love whenever Liam shows his arrow tattoo, and that is why one of our favorite Liam TV looks is from the Today Show. It is definitely one of his most iconic outfits.

Harry

We are OBSESSED with Harry’s TV outfits! Remember when Harry looked like an actual prince at the American Music Awards? One of our other favorites is Harry’s floral suit that he wore for his final performance on The X Factor before the break!

These pictures definitely brought back memories of us watching the boys perform live on TV. While we wait for them to perform on the stage together once again, we will support the boys as individual artists and performers.
