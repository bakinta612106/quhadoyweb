Efe Aydan Biography

Efe Aydan is one of the most popular and richest Basketball Player who was born on May 4, 1955 in Ankara, Ankara, Turkey.

With Turkey’s national team, he won the gold medal at the 1981 Balkan Games, and the gold medal at the 1987 Mediterranean Games. On the club level, he won the Turkish League championship 6 times. Aydan was also the first Turkish basketball player to play in the FIBA European Selection (the All-European Team), in 1981, where he displayed a memorable performance in the game against Joventut Badalona.

The ambitious Aydan started playing basketball in the youth ranks of Galatasaray, when he was 16 years old. Because of his talent, he was discovered by Eczacıbaşı, and he played there for a couple of years. He earned him a place in the FIBA European Selection (All-European Team), in 1981. He retired from playing basketball in 1992, while a member of the Turkish club Tofaş.
