Whats the difference between a cocktail and a margarita?

Publish date: 2024-09-07

As nouns the difference between cocktail and margarita is that cocktail is a mixed alcoholic beverage while margarita is a cocktail made with tequila, an orange-flavoured liqueur, and lemon or lime juice, often served with salt encrusted on the rim of the glass.

Where does the margarita cocktail come from?

David Daniel Danny Negrete is said to have created the drink in 1936 when he was the manager of Hotel Garci Crespo in Tehuacn, Puebla, Mexico. His girlfriend, Margarita, apparently liked salt in her drinks and the story goes that he created the drink for her as a present.
