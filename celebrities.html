Lucy Hale News - Us Weekly

Publish date: 2024-08-30

Name:Lucy Hale

Hometown:Memphis, Tennessee

Relationship:Single

Birthday:June 14, 1989

About

Lucy Hale landed her breakthrough role as Aria Montgomery on ABC Family’s (now Freeform’s) Pretty Little Liars. The hit show ran from 2010 to 2017 and also featured Ashley Benson, Shay Mitchell and Troian Bellisario.

Hale starred on one season of The CW’s Life Sentence in 2018 and starred in A Cinderella Story: Once Upon a Song (2011), Truth or Dare (2018), Dude (2018), Fantasy Island (2020) and The Hating Game (2021). She also cohosted Dick Clark New Year’s Rockin’ Eve with Ryan Seacrest from 2016 to 2021.

Hale dated her Wizards of Waverly Place costar David Henrie from 2007 to 2009. She has also been romantically linked to Chris Zylka, Graham Rogers, country singer Joel Crouse, drummer Adam Pitts and Anthony Kalabretta.
