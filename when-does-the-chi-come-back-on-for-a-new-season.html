When Does The Chi Come Back On For A New Season?

Publish date: 2024-08-16

It is expected that Chi Season 7 will be released in late 2025 or by early 2026, although the official release date for the next season of The Chi is yet to be announced.

Currently, there are six seasons of The Chi, and fans are psyched to know how soon the next season can be released. Since its premiere season was aired in 2017, this coming-of-age drama series has fast become a fans’ favorite, earning a string of nominations and awards along the line. Find out more about when the new season of The Chi will come back on.

When is The Chi New Season Out?

Between 2025 and 2026. The official date for the release of The Chi’s new season has not been announced yet. However, given that the last season was released in August 2023, fans can expect the 7th season of The Chi to be aired sometime between 2025 and 2026.

A Summary of The Chi

The Chi is a coming-of-age drama created by Lena Waithe. It revolves around the lives of residents of a neighborhood on the south side of Chicago in everyday life.

Some of the major characters in the show include young men who are just trying to make a living, single mothers trying to juggle working with taking care of their children, young boys with no proper paternal guidance, and older citizens just keeping an eye on things from their front porches.

However, behind this regular neighborhood, The Chi presents viewers with a peek into the darker sides of the Chicago south side. It shows how a single event can upturn the lives of different individuals, and the simplest decisions can have life-threatening consequences.

The Showtime Original series was first aired on January 7, 2018, and 6 years down the line, it has garnered great recognition and several awards. Currently, The Chi has a total of 6 seasons and 50 episodes.

What Day Does The Chi Come On?

The new episodes of The Chi can be streamed on Fridays and Sundays on two different platforms. Subscribed streamers can stream the new episodes on Fridays on Paramount+ and Sundays on Showtime.

Where Can I Watch The Chi?

The Chi is available for streaming and watching on several platforms. Subscribed users can stream The Chi on Showtime or Paramount+. Other interested viewers can purchase and stream this iconic series on Amazon Prime Video, iTunes, Google Play, FuboTV, and Vudu.
