Strictly Come Dancing fans have theory about Dan Walker's lobster costume and why it wasn't 'Hallowe

Publish date: 2024-08-18

It was Spooky Come Dancing with an eclectic Halloween special in last night’s special episode of the BBC dancing competition.

Halloween Week is one of the most anticipated dates in the Strictly calendar as the wardrobe and makeup departments go all out with an array of fabulously fiendish looks themed around the couples’ routines.

The opening dance saw the likes of Voldemort, Pinocchio and Harley Quinn dancing together and fans were full of praise for the teams behind the scenes who dressed up the pairs and judges for fright night.

However, BBC presenter Dan Walker and his professional dance partner Nadia’s choice of costume raised a few eyebrows and prompted some interesting theories from fans.

The pair performed a jive to The B-52’s Rock Lobster and were aptly dressed as lobsters but fans are convinced there was a deeper reason for their non-Halloween choice of costume.

Fans are convinced the reason Dan didn’t dress up as anything spooky is due to being a devout Christian and therefore doesn’t celebrate Halloween.

Many took to Twitter to share their theories with some even hitting out at the Sheffield born star for not complying with the Halloween theme.

One viewer questioned: “How is a lobster halloweeny?”

A second tweeted: “I’m really struggling to see the connection between Halloween and lobsters.”

A third added: “Dance not too bad considering but song choice and costume awful. Sorry.”

However, Dan’s army of fans were out in force to defend him.

One fan tweeted: “Ridiculous that people were angry that Dan and Nadiya had gone for the lobster theme.

“I have a lot of admiration and respect for Dan standing up for his Christian faith and not following the typical Halloween theme.

“Hope your family enjoy the Light Party!”

Another added: “Ignore the weird angry comments… You brighten up the Strictly dancefloor and that’s all that matters.”

“Love watching you as without any history of dance training you have improved so much, loved the lobsters,” said another supportive fan.

Dan addressed the comments and thanked viewers for their ongoing support, acknowledging the somewhat “weird and angry” comments.

He wrote: “So many amazing comments about last night (& a few weird angry ones) Thanks for voting. I know I’m not the best dancer but I’m having a wonderful time learning from @NadiyaBychkova. So much quality on #Strictly this year. I voted for @RoseAylingEllis who is simply tremendous.”

Strictly Come Dancing will return tonight with the results show as two couples will face the dreaded dance off.
