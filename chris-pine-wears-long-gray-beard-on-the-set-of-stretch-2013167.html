Chris Pine Wears Long Gray Beard on the Set of Stretch

Publish date: 2024-08-31

Talk about a movie makeover! Chris Pine looked noticeably different on the Hollywood set of his new movie, Stretch, on Monday, July 15. The actor, 32, stepped out of his trailer wearing a long beard that featured a gray streak down the middle. Pine, who donned a New York Yankees hat, showed off his muscular arms in a tight gray tank top.

Stretch tells the story of a chauffeur whose billionaire boss makes his life a living hell. Slated for release on March 21, 2014, the comedic thriller is being directed by Joe Carnahan and stars Pine, David Hasselhoff, Brooklyn Decker, Patrick Wilson, Ray Liotta and Ed Helms.
