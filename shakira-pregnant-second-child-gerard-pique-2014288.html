Shakira's Pregnant! Singer to Have Second Child With Gerard Pique

Publish date: 2024-09-05

Update: On Thursday, Aug. 27, Shakira tweeted: "Yes, we are expecting our second baby!! Thank you all for your well wishes!"

—

Baby numero dos! Colombian singing sensation Shakira is pregnant with her second child, she confirmed to the Spanish version of Cosmopolitan. The Voice coach is expecting baby No. 2 with her soccer star boyfriend Gerard Pique, whom she met while promoting the 2010 World Cup anthem “Waka Waka (This Time For Africa).” 

The couple welcomed their first son Milan, 18 months, in January 2013.

“If it wasn’t for the World Cup, my son Milan would not be here,” the international pop star recently told the AP before performing her new 2014 World Cup single “La La La (Brazil 2014)” at the final game on Sunday, July 13. See the cutest pics of Shakira and Milan at the World Cup.

Both Pique and baby Milan appeared in her soccer-centric music video for the new single. The song’s release comes just after the noted humanitarian’s self-titled tenth studio album was released in March, debuting at no. 2 on the Billboard charts. 

Shakira has been very open about her desire to have a big family, telling Latina magazine in April, “If it weren’t because of my music projects, I would be pregnant already. I would love to have eight or nine kids with Gerard — my own futbol team.”
