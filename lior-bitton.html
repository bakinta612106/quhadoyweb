Quick Facts

Lior Bitton Birthday

Lior Bitton was born on December 23, 1973. He was born in Herzliya, Israel. As of 2023, he is 50 years old.

Career

Born and raised in Herzliya, Israel, Lior Bitton served as a soldier in the Israel Defense Forces (IDF). Later, he started his diamond business in Israel itself. 

However, he found success as a businessman, primarily in the USA. Presently, Lior Bitton owns the Pacific Diamonds and Gems store, which is located in Westminster. 

Additionally, Lior Bitton starred in the television documentary, Just Jenna as Rabbi Moray. The T.V. show focused on Lior Bitton’s partner, Jenna Jameson’s life as a former pornographic actress and her later conversion to Judaism.

Legal Issues and Controversies

In January 2010, Lior faced charges of insurance fraudulence and was arrested. Bitton clarified that the insurance amount was based on a false theft report. 

Later, the Israeli businessman filed a complaint stating that one of his couriers from his Pacific Diamonds and Gems store had been stolen. The package containing 20 pieces of valuables also included a 4.21-carat diamond. 

As a result, Lior demanded a total of USD 99 thousand from the insurance company Berkley Asset Protection. However, it was later revealed that Lior flew to Israel with the insurance money.

He was found trying to sell the identical diamonds he claimed to be stolen. The diamonds were sent to the GIA (Gemological Institute of America) to verify their authenticity by the buyer. 

As per the GIA certification report, the gems’ properties and features were the same as those of the stolen ones. Additionally, Lior’s fingerprints were also found all over the diamonds.

In 2011, the businessman was taken into custody. As per the court hearings, he was sentenced to three years of formal probation, five days in jail, and 90 days of home confinement. 

Lior Bitton was also made to return the insurance money to Berkley Asset Protection. Later, Lior got bail in exchange for USD 100 thousand.
