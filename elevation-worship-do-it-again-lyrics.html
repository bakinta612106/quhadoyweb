About

Elevation Worship

Elevation Worship is a contemporary worship music collective from Elevation Church in Charlotte, North Carolina. The collective leads worship in weekend church services at Elevation Church, as well as performing concerts and tours around the United States. The collective has sold over 2.5 million albums in the United States.

Elevation Worship was founded in 2007 by Steven Furtick, the senior pastor of Elevation Church. The band’s first album, The Sound, was released in 2007. The album was a success, and it helped to establish Elevation Worship as a leading force in the contemporary worship music scene.

Elevation Worship has released a total of 11 studio albums, including There Is a Cloud (2017), Graves Into Gardens (2018), and No One Like You (2020). The band has also released a number of live albums and EPs.

Elevation Worship’s music is characterized by its catchy melodies, uplifting lyrics, and powerful vocals. The band’s songs are often used in worship services around the world, and they have been featured on numerous Christian radio stations.

Elevation Worship has won numerous awards for their music, including a Grammy Award for Best Contemporary Christian Music Album for their 2018 album Graves Into Gardens. The band has also been nominated for numerous other awards, including Billboard Music Awards and Dove Awards.
