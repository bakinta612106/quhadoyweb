Ana de Armas NC-17 Movie Blonde Gets First Trailer &amp; Her Transformation Into Marilyn Monroe

Publish date: 2024-09-02

The stunning first trailer for Blonde, the NC-17 biopic starring Ana de Armas as Hollywood icon Marilyn Monroe, has just debuted online.

The movie is based on the bestselling novel by Joyce Carol Oates and follows Marilyn’s life from her childhood as Norma Jean to the blonde bombshell on screen, plus, follows her romantic attachments.

The supporting cast features Bobby Cannavale, Adrien Brody, Julianne Nicholson, Xavier Samuel and Evan Williams.

“The film moves along with her feelings and her experiences. There are moments when we are inside of her body and mind, and this will give the audience an opportunity to experience what it was like to be Norma and Marilyn at the same time,” Ana said of the film.

The film will become available on Netflix on September 28, 2022.
