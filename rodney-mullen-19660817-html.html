John Rodney Mullen is an American skateboarder, businessman, inventor and speaker, independent and street skateboarder. He is considered by many to be the most influential street skater in the history of the sport, credited with inventing many tricks including ollie on the plane, kickflip, heelflip, no and 360-flip. Hence, he has been called the "Godfather of street skateboarding". Rodney Mullen won his first world skateboarding championship at the age of 14; Over the next decade, he won 34 out of 35 freestyle competitions, setting up the most successful competitive race in the sport's history. Over the next few years, he switched from freelance to street skateboarding, adapting his accumulated freelance skills to the street and inventing or developing additional tricks in the process, for example. such as sliding primo, darkslides and casper.
