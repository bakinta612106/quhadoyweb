Rihanna Owned the Met Gala 2015 With the Longest Train Ever: Photos

Publish date: 2024-08-21

Bitch better have some help with that! Rihanna shut down the red carpet at the Met Gala 2015 on Monday, May 4, in a yellow, fur-trimmed cape with a train that required the help of at least three men.

“Present!!” Rihanna perfectly captioned an image of her train taking over the staircase at the fete on Instagram.

RiRi completed her showstopping ensemble with an intricate headpiece. Underneath her cape, the star donned a pink corseted outfit which showed off her ample cleavage.

Rihanna’s impressive cape was designed by Chinese designer Guo Pei, a perfect fit for the evening that was themed “China: Through the Looking Glass.”

After making her way into the bash, Rihanna changed into a more manageable red strapless dress.

Last year, the fashion icon went braless and bared her toned stomach in a fitted white long-sleeved gown.
