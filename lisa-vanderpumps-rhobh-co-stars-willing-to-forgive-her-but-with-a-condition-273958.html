Lisa Vanderpumps RHOBH Co-Stars Willing To Forgive Her But With A Condition!

Publish date: 2024-08-22

As the Real Housewives of Beverly Hills reunion is approaching, it looks like the RHOBH stars are open to forgiving Lisa Vanderpump. As you may know, the whole cast hasn’t had the best of relationships with her because of the Puppygate scandal.

But now that the reunion is right around the corner, they might be willing to forgive and forget – with one condition!

One source tells HollywoodLife that ‘The ladies of RHOBH are open to forgiving Lisa Vanderpump if she apologizes, plain and simple. They’ve collectively discussed it and they all feel like they can move forward from this, especially Dorit, if Lisa comes to the reunion, first and foremost to face them, and say she’s sorry and admits her involvement in puppygate.’

‘They all reall want her to return and to be friends again. The other ladies have even opened up Kyle [Richards] to this, despite her telling friends she’s done with Lisa’s friendship for good,’ the insider went on to share with the news outlet.

As fans of the show know, the drama started when Dorit Kemsley adopted a dog from Lisa’s shelter, Vanderpump Dogs.

However, after the pup allegedly bit her husband and their kids, she gave it away to someone else, who also gave it away!

In the end, the dog ended up in another shelter, without Lisa or even Dorit knowing.

Lisa eventually learned about this and got the dog back to her organization.

Vanderpump was then accused of trying to make Dorit look bad and cause drama between her and  Kyle and Teddi Mellencamp.

When the two visited Lisa’s shelter, one employee brought out the animal and revealed to them that it was almost killed in a random shelter and Dorit was to blame since she did not handle the whole situation correctly.
