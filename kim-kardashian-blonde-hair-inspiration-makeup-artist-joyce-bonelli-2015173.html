Kim Kardashian's Blonde Hair Inspiration: Makeup Artist Joyce Bonelli

Publish date: 2024-08-31

Kiki's true muse has arrived!

When Kim Kardashian debuted platinum blonde hair in Paris on March 5, the whole world (aka the Internet) seemed shocked—everyone, except one person that is: celeb makeup artist Joyce Bonelli, who says she's the reason the reality star lightened up.

"Still playing 'single white females.' Opposites don't attract. Bleached blondes have the most fun," began Bonelli, who also works with Khloe Kardashian, Kendall Jenner, and Gigi Hadid, in an epic Instagram post on Tuesday, March 17, that featured the matching blondes side by side.

The makeup artist went on to say that she's thrilled that after two years of trying to convince the Keeping Up With the Kardashians star to go bold and blonde (with the help of Kanye West), Kardashian, 34, finally listened.
