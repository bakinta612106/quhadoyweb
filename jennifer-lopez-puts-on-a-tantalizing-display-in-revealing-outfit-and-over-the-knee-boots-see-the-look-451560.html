Jennifer Lopez Puts On A Tantalizing Display In Revealing Outfit And Over-The-Knee Boots See The

Publish date: 2024-08-11

Jennifer Lopez is putting on a tantalizing display in a revealing dress and a pair of over-the-knee boots from her shoe collection DSW. Jennifer looked incredible and fans can’t believe that she is 51-years-old. Jennifer posed on a pool table while wearing a silky, red gown. The dress matched perfectly with her boots that featured stiletto heels and reached over her knees.

The boots are called the Azzala and are made from faux suede. The heel is four and a half inches high and the shoes retail for approximately $99.99.

Jennifer’s hair looked gorgeous and she wore it honey-blonde and curly. She wore it piled up on her head in loose, face-framing tendrils.

Jennifer stretched out on the pool table with her dress that featured a mock turtleneck. The dress has long sleeves and a thigh-high slit that showed off her thigh and leg.
