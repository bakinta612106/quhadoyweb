KOI KNOWLES WANTS TO BE A SINGER LIKE HER HALF SISTER BEYONCE

Publish date: 2024-08-21

Koi Knowles is a four-year-old model and actress. According to her mom, Taqoya Branscomb, Koi also has a passion for singing and dancing like her half-sisters Beyoncé and Solange.

The famous sisters’ father, Matthew Knowles, was recently named as the father of Koi in court documents filed in May of this year.  Taqoya Branscomb says that there is a paternity test that states  Mr. Knowles is the father of her daughter by a 99.9 percent certainty.

In an interview with Inside Edition, the former lingerie model revealed photos of her child and the details of a paternity test.

She said the results were 99.998% positive in favour of 63-year-old Knowles being the father.

Branscomb said she was shocked to discover she was pregnant. “‘He’d had a vasectomy. Or at least that was what I was told. You can’t deny it once DNA tests come out 99.9% that you are the father,” she added.

The 30-year-old from Texas, who works as a realtor, said her daughter has a talent for singing and dancing, and she hopes she might meet her half-sister someday.

“They are half sisters and maybe one day down the line that can be made possible,” she said (Source).

Matthew also has a son named Nixon Knowles.  Taqoya’s interview will air on Thursday, Oct. 30, on Inside Edition
