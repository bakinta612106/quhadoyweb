Claire Danes Plastic Surgery Before and After

Publish date: 2024-08-19

Claire Catherine Danes was born on April 12, 1979 in Manhattan, New York. She first came to prominence as Angela Chase in the TV series My So-Called Life and made her film debut with 1994’s Little Women. Two years after, she gained mainstream fame for her portrayal of Juliet Capulet in 1996’s Romeo + Juliet.

She continued starring in several well-known films, including The Rainmaker (1997), Les Misérables (1998), The Hours (2002), Terminator 3: Rise of the Machines (2003) and many others. She also gained stardom for her critically acclaimed performance in 2010’s Temple Grandin and in the TV series Homeland.

Has Claire Danes had Plastic Surgery?

The Homeland star has been rumored of going under the knife for breast implants, as well as for receiving Botox injections. When asked about the ongoing rumors, Danes clarified that she has never had any plastic surgery of any sort. Her open-mindedness on the subject has always put her on the spotlight as someone who would undergo some sort of cosmetic operation any time soon.

The 35-year-old actress said in an interview that as of now, she’s happy with how she looks and is thankful that her body is forgiving and that she can get away with a lot because she’s still young. She hinted that she is considering plastic surgery in the future, depending on how things will turn out for her.

She also acknowledges the fact that women nowadays have so many choices with how they want to preserve and maintain their face and body with products that have scientific basis. For her, as long as one knows potential risks and is being watched closely by a doctor, she’s okay with plastic surgery.
