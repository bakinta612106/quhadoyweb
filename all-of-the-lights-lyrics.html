About

Kanye West is a highly successful American rapper, songwriter, record producer, and fashion designer. He was born on June 8, 1977 in Atlanta, Georgia, but raised in Chicago, Illinois.

West first gained fame with his debut album “The College Dropout” in 2004, which received critical acclaim and commercial success. He has since released several other successful albums, including “Late Registration,” “Graduation,” “My Beautiful Dark Twisted Fantasy,” and “Yeezus.”

In addition to his music career, Kanye West has also made headlines for his public persona, outspoken views and controversies. He has been known to make controversial statements or engage in provocative behavior, such as interrupting Taylor Swift’s acceptance speech at the MTV Video Music Awards in 2009 and wearing a “Make America Great Again” hat during a meeting with President Donald Trump in 2018.

West has also ventured into fashion design, launching his Yeezy brand in collaboration with Adidas in 2015. The line features sneakers, clothing, and accessories worn by celebrities and sold out within minutes of release.
