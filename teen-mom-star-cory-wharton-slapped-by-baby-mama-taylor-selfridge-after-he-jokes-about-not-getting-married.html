Publish date: 2024-09-05

TEEN Mom star Cory Wharton was slapped by his baby mama Taylor Selfridge after he had joked about not getting married. 

Cory, 30, and  Taylor, 27, filmed themselves standing in front of a mansion.

During the Instagram Story, a crowd had gathered as the couple said cheers with their champagne glasses. 

Taylor stunned in her black dress as Cory wore a white shirt buttoned up to the collar.

Wedding music was playing in the background. 

The Teen Mom star said: “Somebody tied the knot…not me!”

The MTV star started laughing as Taylor rolled her eyes at him. 

She then slapped him across the side of his face. 

The Teen Mom OG star also captioned the vid: “Love you bae.”

The reality star couple – who met on Ex On The Beach – are the parents to their one-year-old daughter Mila, while Cory is also the father to his four-year-old daughter Ryder with his ex Cheyenne Floyd, 28.

DID THEY BREAK UP?

Back in June, fans were convinced Cory and Taylor had split.

Fans started speculating that the pair might have parted ways after a series of suspicious social media posts.

The speculation came after one viewer claimed the TV star had been “weirdly quiet on social media.”

Meanwhile, Taylor had failed to mention her boyfriend after giving a present to a friend.

THE COMMENTS

The user wrote on Reddit: “Cue the ‘no one cares’ comments, but I have suspicions Taylor and Cory broke up…”

“Cory has been weirdly quiet on social media and Taylor shared this of her congratulating a friend on the birth of her daughter and no mention of Cory (or even Ryder) on the card.”

A second user replied: “Omg you guys we called it. How many months after Mila’s birth did we say they would split?!?”

A third commentator wrote: “I wouldn’t be surprised, Corey will never be a one-woman man.”

Another person pointed out: “The only one I feel bad for is Mila. Cory will have 5 other baby mommas in the future.”

Back in April, the reality stars had celebrated Mila’s first birthday with a Toy Story themed-party.

The TV personality and his baby mama wore matching tee-shirts with their kids as they all dressed up for the celebration

THE MORNING HIKE

Fans blasted him for creating content for social media to earn himself “likes/views.”
