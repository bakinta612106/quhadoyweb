Jar Of Hearts Lyrics

Publish date: 2024-09-05

Jar of Hearts is a popular song by American singer-songwriter Christina Perri, released in 2010 as the lead single from her debut album, “Lovestrong.”

The song was a commercial success, reaching the top 5 on the Billboard Hot 100 chart and receiving multi-platinum certifications in several countries.
