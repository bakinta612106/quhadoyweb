Tallulah Willis Shaves Her Head Just Like Mom Demi Moore in G.I. Jane

Publish date: 2024-09-07

Just call her G.I. Tallulah Belle!

Tallulah Willis paid homage to one of mom Demi Moore‘s most iconic roles, G.I. Jane, with her shocking new haircut: a completely shaved head.

Willis, 20, debuted her bold ‘do via her Instagram page on Tuesday, Nov. 18. “November 18th. remember it,” Moore’s youngest daughter (with ex Bruce Willis) captioned a shot in which she modeled her bare head.

Just in case there was any doubt as to who served as her hair-spiration, Willis followed up that post with a pretty picture of her mama with a shaved head, circa her G.I. Jane days in 1997. “mawms is bae,” the aspiring fashion blogger wrote, hashtagging the note, “Thank God good head shapes run in the family.”

Willis made headlines this past summer when she entered rehab to combat body dysmorphia. But according to Rumer Willis, her little sister is back and better than ever. While Tallulah shaved her head, Rumer taped the whole thing, then shared it on her social media pages. “@buuski is a bad ass bitch #GIjane #rawbeauty,” Rumer captioned the clip.
